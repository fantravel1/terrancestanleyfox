Cancer Diagnosis & Treatment

Terry's journey with osteosarcoma - from the first symptoms to the determination that arose from his hospital experience.

Cancer Journey Timeline

November 1976
Terry experiences persistent pain in his right knee, initially attributed to sports injury
Late 1976
Diagnosis: Osteogenic sarcoma (bone cancer) in right leg
March 9, 1977
Right leg amputated 15 cm above the knee at Royal Columbian Hospital
April 1977
Fitted with first prosthetic leg; begins rehabilitation
1977-1978
16 months of chemotherapy treatment
September 1, 1980
Cancer detected in lungs; Marathon of Hope ends
June 28, 1981
Terry passes away at Royal Columbian Hospital
